BMI Calculator

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. The formula is BMI = kg/m2.

BMI categories are:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI of 30 or greater
